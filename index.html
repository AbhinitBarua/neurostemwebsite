About NeuroStem

Enhancing cognitive function through cutting-edge neuroscience

NeuroStem is a groundbreaking system that combines neurofeedback and brain stimulation techniques to enhance cognitive functions in patients with neurological disorders such as stroke, Alzheimer's, and traumatic brain injury.

Our technology provides customized brain exercises based on each patient's recovery stage, promoting faster recovery from neurological impairments and improving cognitive functions.

Innovative Features

Six revolutionary technologies that set NeuroStem apart

Tri-Layer Stimulation System

Three-level feedback system combining brain (EEG), body (haptic), and emotion to promote neuroplasticity

"Mirror Mind" AI Engine

AI digital twin of patient's brain behavior that learns and adapts to optimize therapy

Remote Neuronet: Swarm-Rehab

Multi-user cognitive games promoting social neuroplasticity through patient collaboration

Modular NeuroBox

Low-cost kit for rural use with plug-and-play modules designed for offline operation

Emotionally Intelligent Companion

AI assistant that detects mood and adapts to provide personalized emotional support

SleepSync Neuro Recovery

Promotes neuroplasticity during sleep by detecting sleep phases and providing targeted stimulation
